FAQ

Where and when is the reunion?
May 9th, 2026 from 2PM–6PM at Wachusett Brewing Company, 175 State Rd E, Westminster, MA 01473.
How do I get tickets?
Venmo Dylan Nadeau at @dylanmn. Include the full name of every ticket holder in the Venmo note so we can add you to the list.
How much are tickets?
Early bird tickets are $16 per person through April 9th. After that, tickets are $20 per person through May 9th.
Can I bring a guest?
Yes! Plus ones are welcome. Just purchase a ticket for each guest attending.
What should I wear?
Keep it casual — it's a relaxed hangout at a brewery. Come as you are!
Is there parking?
Yes, free parking is available right at the venue with plenty of space.
Will there be food and drinks?
Food and drinks are available for purchase at the bar. Your ticket covers the venue and event — food and drinks are on your own.
Who do I contact with questions?
Email us at leominsterclassof2016@gmail.com and we'll get back to you!
